OVERVIEW

While working full-time at West Elm, I took on part-time freelance projects to continue growing my skills in construction documentation and learn more about residential design.

I created detailed visual graphics and construction drawings, collaborating with contractors and incorporating revisions based on team feedback and client approvals. I also developed mood boards, material palettes, and design concepts, refining them through iterative reviews to deliver thoughtful, well-executed spaces.
