My name is Elana and I am a Designer

MY DESIGN JOURNEY

I graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design in 2018 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Interior Design.

Started a career in commercial and multi-family design, gaining experience in cross-functional team collaboration and end-to-end project management.


During lockdown,
I did a career shift

During the pandemic, I earned a Master of Professional Studies in UX Design from the Maryland Institute College of Art, where I deepened my understanding of human psychology, design research, and digital product development.

While working on contract projects for e-commerce and healthcare software companies, I built new skill sets in user-centered design, digital tools, and agile project workflows—broadening my perspective as a designer.








MY end Goal

As technology continues to evolve and expand in the role of AI, I'm excited to return to interior design with a refreshed lens. I aim to integrate my creativity, technical expertise, and user-focused mindset into meaningful, human-centered spaces.

My ultimate goal is to join a design firm that specializes in high-end residential and commercial interiors, where I can contribute thoughtfully crafted solutions that enhance the way people live.
