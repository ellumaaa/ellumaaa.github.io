WELCOME TO MY "ABOUT ME"

My name is Elana and I am a UI/UX Designer
based in Seattle, WA. Enjoy some facts about me!











MY VALUES AS A DESIGNER

My creative interests and academic history has led me to investigate and immerse myself on how the power of human-centered design and analytical research can cultivate communities and enhance the human experience by creating memorable experiences that uplifts the human soul every day.










MY DESIGN JOURNEY

In 2017, I graduated from Savannah College of Art and Design with a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Interior Design.

After post-graduation, I worked in theme-park entertainment design, lighting design, and healthcare interior design.


Throughout my life, since I was a child, I always love creating memorable experiences and being intentional when it came to design.

When I was at the peak of my career as an interior designer and geared myself towards being licensed, I realized that was not a career path I wanted to pursue.

I ultimately was not able to hone in the construction in the interior design realm, which is what I loved still, but through self-reflection on what brings me happiness, my curiosity for UX design grew louder, and went back to school to obtain my masters in UX design.
